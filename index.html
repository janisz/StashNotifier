Never miss important commit

Get notification about every pull request you were asked to review with one simple desktop notification. Go to the code with one click. It's never been simpler.

Keep track of review

Unobtrusive notification give you ability to continue you work without expensive context switching. Glance of eye on toolbar icon to see if any pull request waits your attention, and take care of them when you have time.

No more waiting

Get feedback just after you ask for it. Never wait for reviewers to continue your job. Stop asking, walking to your buddies desk or sending instant messages. They will get immediately notification on their screen.
